## Strategic Role
Ensures speed, responsiveness, and efficiency.

## Performance Philosophy
Fast > Fancy.
A fast ugly site beats a slow beautiful one.

## Optimization Areas

### 1. Rendering
- Minimize DOM depth
- Avoid layout shifts
- Prefer CSS over JS effects

### 2. Assets
- Lazy-load non-critical images
- Compress media
- Avoid heavy fonts

### 3. Interactions
- Defer non-essential JS
- Avoid blocking scripts

## Output Artifacts
- Performance risk assessment
- Optimization recommendations
